Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 15, 2026

Sleepy Moon LLC ("Sleepy Moon," "we," "our," or "us") operates the Sleepy Moon mobile application and the website sleepymoon.app (collectively, the "Service"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: Name and email address when you create an account.
  • User Content: Audio recordings and messages you create within the app.
  • Usage Data: Anonymous information about how you interact with the app, such as features used and session length, to help us improve the Service.
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, and unique device identifiers for technical support and app improvement purposes.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, operate, and improve the Sleepy Moon Service.
  • Enable sharing of recordings between family members you invite.
  • Respond to your support requests and communications.
  • Send service-related notifications (e.g., a family member shared a recording with you).

3. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information or recordings with third parties for advertising or marketing purposes. We may share information only in the following limited circumstances:

  • With your family: Recordings are shared only with the family members you explicitly invite.
  • Service providers: Trusted vendors who assist us in operating our infrastructure (e.g., cloud storage), bound by confidentiality obligations.
  • Legal requirements: If required by law or to protect the rights and safety of our users.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your recordings and personal data are stored securely using industry-standard encryption. We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the Service. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us at james@sleepymoon.app.

5. Children's Privacy

The Sleepy Moon app is intended for use by adults (parents and grandparents). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If you believe a child has provided us personal information without parental consent, please contact us immediately.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete your personal information. To exercise these rights, contact us at james@sleepymoon.app.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by posting the new policy on this page with an updated effective date.
