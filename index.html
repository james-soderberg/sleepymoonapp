About Sleepy Moon LLC

Sleepy Moon LLC is an independent software company dedicated to building technology that strengthens family bonds. We believe that a grandparent's voice reading a bedtime story is irreplaceable — and that geography should never stand in the way of that connection.

We are based in the United States and are committed to building simple, trustworthy apps for real families.
